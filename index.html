Welcome to Alpha-Sci

The Reliable Way to Be Better


Our Mission

  • To provide a precise way for fund managers to assess the performance of their traders
  • To provide reliable tools for stock traders to outperform the market average

The Problem

  • Merely 16 out of the largest 100 hedgefunds beat the S&P last year (Forbes)
  • Average blend funds have an alpha of -1.25 (Putnam Investments)
  • 99.4% of mutual funds reported a negative (Journal of Finance)

The Solution

  • A web app plug-in that calculates the alpha of a trade in real time
  • An algorithm that finds better performing stocks within the same sector
  • An algorithm that finds better performing selling intervals within the same holding period

Opportunity

  • The finance industry made up 8% of US GDP in 2012 Alone (Department of Commerce)
  • R&D spending in finance is among the lowest in all industries: 0.4% (National Science Foundation)
  • Hedge funds spend between 300,000 and 1.9 million dollars in technology every year (City Prime Finance)

Competitors

  • Mutual Fund Expert can rank funds on 1,3,5,and 10 year averages of alpha
  • Smartmoney.com ranks mutual funds online
  • The New York Times has a screener for mutual funds that can factor in alphas
