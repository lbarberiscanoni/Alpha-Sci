Our Purpose

We build technology that improves financial performance over time

The Problem

  • Most funds are not able to display effective stock-picking ability
  • 50% of hedge funds reports a negative alpha, and only 16 beat the S&P last year
  • Between 1976 and 2006 only 0.6% of mutual funds exhibited any consistent skill at beating the S&P

Our Solution

  • A plug-in that produces an alpha-analysis for every trade
  • An algorithm that finds better performing stocks within thes same holding period
  • An algorithm that finds more profitable selling times within the same holding period

The Opportunity

  • The financial sector accounted for 8% of US GDP in 2012 alone
  • Each hedge fund spends between $300,000 and $1.9 million on technology
  • The financial industry has one of the lowest (0.4%) R&D spending rates of all industries
